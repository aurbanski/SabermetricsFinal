Search for a baseball player

Our website will generate statistic for you to show you how good your player might be in 5 years. We'll also compare your player to others around the mlb and show you some graphs for comparison.

Why Was It Created?

We wanted to create a statistic that was similar to the 'player type' research that Bill James did. So we decided to create a statistic that might look at how good a player would be in the future. And we thought that it might be interesting to see how players today age compared to older players with advances in medicine and practices around sports.

How Does It Work?

We pulled in a lot of batting data from Fangraphs batting in order to calculate the statistic for each player and standardize it across players.
